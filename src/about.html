About

Who We Are

PrimaDIY is an online retail store with a can-do attitude when it comes to making fun jewelry, kids crafts and seasonal craft projects. Our in-house designers are always coming up with brilliant ways to merge original style and self-expression into interesting new looks! We know that the needs of the crafting community are always changing, so staying current with the growing demand for evolving designs helps us to remain stocked with the latest in top quality products.

The New Way to craft. design. create.

Don’t miss our exclusive library of project ideas! They’re sure to make you want to clear some space on your crafting table. All of our projects also include a free pattern and list of materials, so you can easily share what you’ve created with a friend, or save the instructions and complete it later! Connect with us on Social Media to experience the support that comes from joining a community of crafters that treasure individuality and #BeadAsYouAre. Thanks for being a part of a team of inspired creators, we can’t wait to see what you decide to make next!
