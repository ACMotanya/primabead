Necklace Instructions

  1. Slide (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, and (1) silver beehive bead onto (1) silver eye pin and form a loop
  2. Slide (3) large dark blue gemstone beads onto each of (3) silver eye pins and form a loop. Slide (4) light blue gemstone beads onto each of (4) silver eye pins and form a loop.
  3. Connect the beaded links in Steps #1 and #2 together in this order: (1) purple bicone link, (1) light blue gemstone link, (1) purple bicone link, (1) dark blue gemstone link, (1) purple bicone link, (1) light blue gemstone link, (1) purple bicone link, (1) dark blue gemstone link, (1) purple bicone link, (1) light blue gemstone link, (1) purple bicone link, (1) dark blue gemstone link, (1) purple mirror bicone link, (1) light blue gemstone link, and (1) purple bicone link. Connect the last loop on each end of the strand to (1) 6mm jump ring on each side.
  4. Cut (1) 16” length of 7-strand beading wire and string on: (3) purple mirror bicones, (1) silver beehive bead, (3) purple mirror bicones, (1) silver beehive bead. Continue this pattern until you have a total (13) sets of (3) purple mirror bicones each and (12) silver beehive beads. Crimp each end of this strand, above the strand from Step #3, onto the same 6mm silver jump rings used in Step #3.
  5. Cut (1) 14” length of 7-strand beading wire and string on the following: (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) blue rubber coated glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) light blue glass bead, (1) beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) blue rubber coated glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) light blue glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) blue rubber coated glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) light blue glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) blue rubber coated blue glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) light blue glass bead, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, (1) silver beehive bead, (1) blue rubber coated glass bead, and (1) silver beehive bead. Crimp each end of this strand, above the strand from Step #4, onto the same 6mm silver jump rings used in Steps #3 and #4.

Earring Instructions

  1. Slide (1) eye pin through (1) silver head pin and form a loop.
  2. Slide (1) silver beehive bead, (1) purple mirror bicone, and (1) silver beehive bead onto (1) silver eye pin and forma loop. Connect this link to the head pin loop in Step #1.
  3. Connect the top loop of the eye pin to the earring wire to complete one earring.
  4. Repeat Steps #1-3 for the matching earring.
